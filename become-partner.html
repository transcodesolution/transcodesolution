Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses can join the partner program?

We welcome IT service providers, consultancies, resellers, and system integrators across industries. If your business is focused on delivering technology solutions, you’re a great fit for our program.

How does the revenue-sharing model work?

Our revenue-sharing model varies depending on the type of partnership. We offer competitive commissions for referral partners and profit-sharing models for reseller and strategic alliance partners. Contact us for more specific details.

What kind of support will I receive as a partner?

As a partner, you’ll receive comprehensive support, including access to a dedicated partner account manager, technical training, marketing resources, and ongoing technical support for your clients.

Can I offer Transcodezy’s services under my own brand?

Yes, through our white-label program, you can rebrand and resell our IT solutions under your company’s name, maintaining full control over your client relationships.
